# Terra Farming — Highlight Voiceover Script

Condensed from the 40-slide walkthrough into the strongest features. ~3 minutes at a natural pace. Each beat is written to stand alone as its own short clip (for hover-to-play tiles) or play back-to-back as one reel.

Recommended voice: calm, confident, mid-pace. ElevenLabs "Adam" or "Daniel" both fit a premium product tone.

---

## 01 — Opening
Terra Farming is the Philippines' first farm-to-table marketplace — connecting highland Benguet farmers directly to buyers nationwide. From dirt to dessert. Fifty-plus partner farms, a thousand-plus customers, twenty-four-hour delivery.

## 02 — The Differentiator: Multi-Farm Price Compare
Here's what makes Terra different. Most marketplaces show you one price. Terra shows you every farm supplying the same product — side by side — with a Best Price badge. Baguio Cabbage from four farms, fifty-eight to sixty-five pesos a kilo. The buyer compares, then chooses. That's wholesale-grade transparency for a consumer order.

## 03 — Compliance-First
Terra is built for a regulated market from the ground up. KYC and KYB verification before full platform access. Every farm is ATI or PhilGAP certified, with its LSA certificate number visible on every card. Full traceability — soil to shelf.

## 04 — The Terra Journey
Soil to table in four steps. Partner farms sit fifteen hundred meters up in the Benguet highlands. Produce is harvested at dawn, at peak freshness — no cold storage, no warehouses. It's dispatched within hours through GRAB and Lalamove, direct to your door.

## 05 — Business Centre
This is the Super Admin command centre — the entire platform managed from a single pane of glass. System-wide KPIs at a glance: members, fee volume, wallet balances, orders, and live logistics.

## 06 — The Fail-Safe
This is the mathematical guarantee. If commission payouts ever approach seventy-five percent of the compensation pool, the cycle value automatically decreases to protect it. The platform can never pay out more than what comes in. It's enforced in code — not policy.

## 07 — Multi-Role System
One account, many roles. A single user can be a buyer, a farmer, a driver, an admin, and an affiliate — all at once, with no re-registration. Terra is built for how people actually participate in a supply chain.

## 08 — Commission Engine
The compensation rules are hardcoded into the engine. One peso of Terra Fee equals one BV. Only the Terra Fee is commissionable — the farmer's subtotal and the delivery fee never generate volume. Binary matching, one-to-one, at ten percent of the lesser leg.

## 09 — Financial Control
Every peso is auditable. An append-only ledger — no hard deletes, ever. Every wallet adjustment requires dual approval. Every action is written to an immutable audit trail, and every running balance traces back to its source event.

## 10 — Network & Growth
An interactive binary-tree genealogy map — click to inspect, drag to pan, zoom through the network depth. Unique referral links track the entire funnel: clicks, registrations, and activations. Every activation creates a commission event and a new tree node, automatically.

## 11 — Close
Terra Farming. Live in production. Designed, built, and shipped end-to-end by Tailored Tech Solutions.
